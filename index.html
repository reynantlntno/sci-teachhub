MOTION

Motion is the change of position of an object with regard to time.

ENERGY

Energy is defined as the ability to do work, which is the ability to exert a force causing displacement of an object.

WORK

Work is a measure of energy transfer that takes place when an object is moved over a distance by an external force that is applied in the displacement direction.

POWER

Power is the rate at which work is done or energy is transferred in a unit of time.

LIGHT

Light is an electromagnetic radiation consisting of oscillating electric and magnetic field that travels through empty space.

SOUND

Sound is a mechanical wave that results from the back and forth vibration of the particles of the medium through which the sound wave is moving.

HEAT

Heat is an energy that is transferred from one body to another as the result of a difference in temperature.

ELECTRICITY

Electricity is a form of energy that results from the flow of charged particles.
